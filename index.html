Privacy

Effective Date: February 2026

YAWA NOAA does not collect, sell, or share any personal data with the developer.

The app may request access to your device’s precise or approximate location in order to retrieve nearby weather conditions, forecasts, and alerts from public weather services such as NOAA (weather.gov). Your location is used only to make these requests and is not stored or transmitted anywhere else.

All calculations and processing occur locally on your device. No accounts, analytics, tracking technologies, or advertising SDKs are used.

Third-Party Services

Weather data is retrieved directly from public providers such as NOAA (weather.gov). YAWA NOAA does not control and is not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

Data Usage

YAWA NOAA does not collect or retain any user data on external servers. Weather data is fetched directly from the providers listed above based on your location.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
